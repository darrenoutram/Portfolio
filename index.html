WHO AM I?

I have always had a passion for creating new things and solving problems by developing my skills. I have been inspired to persue a career in design since I was young, watching my Mum paint as a Freelance muralist. “After Graduating From Ravensbourne University”, I Have Been Actively Involved with several varying projects which included desining for non-profitable organisations and small businesses.

My Main Speciality is Branding And Web Design and Development i mostly use HTML And CSS to create website and use the adobe creative cloud to create other content needed, i can also film and have experience filming from doing live streams at events like TeenTech to documenting the Mozzila Festival in 2016.

When I’m not designing I’m probably on a walk, At a art exhibition or I do like Playing Guitar/Piano.

EDUCATION

While I was at Plymouth college of art I learned all of the skills to become a graphic designer and illustrator ranging from setting up the proper print settings for certain graphics and the anatomy of typography I also had detailed drawing lessons that improved my drawing skills.

I also learned how to use programs like Cinema 4d, adobe creative suite, affinity Designer and I also learned non-digital skills like making hand rendered fonts and using traditional Japanese brushes.

During my time at Ravensbourne have learnt a lot of things and grew a passion for coding I have been able to improve my skills as a designer by being challenged with industry style briefs, through Ravensbourne I learnt not on how to User friendly web pages and apps using mainly HTML, CSS, and jquery and found I have a huge passion for User Experience design.

I also learned how to set out strategic social marketing strategies and different management programs like Trello and GitHub, Evernote.
